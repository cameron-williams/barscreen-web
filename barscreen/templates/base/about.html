About

Our team is building the next generation of restaurant entertainment

BarScreen is an industry innovator in the fast paced and growing smart TV industry. Our mission is to provide innovative technology solutions to solve the problems for restaurants and pubs. We provide one platform that creates an extremely easy to use experience for managers: one smart TV channel, one dashboard, every screen. Our team is dedicated to creating best in class poducts and backing it with excellent support.

We are happy to be headquartered in Victoria, British Columbia. Our little hometown has one of the highest restaurant density, which makes it the perfect location to launch our product. Our services are available across North America, so please reach out or sign up if you would like to know more.
